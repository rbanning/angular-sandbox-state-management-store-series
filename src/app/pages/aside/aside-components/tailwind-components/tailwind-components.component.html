Surfaces

We use surfaces to show depth (really elevation). By moving content up the z-axis (elevating it), we are signaling that the content is more important. The higher the content, the more important the content.

We borrow the concept of surfaces from Material Design and incorporate our different theme colors {{colors.join(', ')}} to provide a variety of surfaces that range in elevation ({{surfaceSizes.join(', ')}}).
